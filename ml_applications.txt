Applications of Machine Learning

Machine Learning is widely used across industries to automate tasks and
gain insights.

Major Applications:

Healthcare: - Disease prediction - Medical image analysis - Personalized
treatment

Finance: - Fraud detection - Credit scoring - Stock prediction

Technology: - Chatbots - Search engines - Autonomous vehicles

Business: - Customer segmentation - Demand forecasting - Marketing
optimization

Future Scope: Machine learning is expected to play a major role in
robotics, smart cities, climate prediction, and advanced AI systems.
